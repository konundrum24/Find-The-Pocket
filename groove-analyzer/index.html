No click. Just play and the app figures out your tempo and pocket. Play for at least 15 seconds.

To analyze a song, play it from another device near your mic.

Place your phone/mic right next to the speaker. Even a few feet of distance introduces room reflections that shift timing by 10–15ms — enough to change your results.

Use a full-range speaker, not a phone speaker. Phone speakers lack bass, so kick drum and bass guitar onsets won’t be detected reliably.
